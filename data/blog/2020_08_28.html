Tonight I want to talk about another application that could be helpful in my use of RSS in Lightning: newsboat.

newsboat is a command line RSS/ATOM reader. It might not process images like some RSS Readers in a GUI, but I'm content with that. RSS is supposed to be simplistic. Basically a newsreader providing text and links.

LightniNG will likely have the same functionally as newsboat or the Iowa Environment Mesonet (a.k.a. Mesonet). Mesonet aggregates messages from the National Weather Service, but this data is more geared toward scientists, meteorologists, and storm chasers. My application is more geared toward the general public.

Then there is the notification feature, which thanks to some recent upgrades a the United States Geological Survey, their Earthquake webpage now has notification features (which are new) in addition to the RSS feeds they've had for about a couple decades now.
